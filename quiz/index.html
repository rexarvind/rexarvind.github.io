Annual Developer Quiz

Welcome, this quiz is a quick way to refresh your web development knowledge and learn something new at the same time. To make this quiz interesting we are shuffling questions and answers both at the server side and the client side; and we will give you certificate of successful completion and show your scores here. More questions are added every week and as JavaScript is the leader in web development, more questions will be related to it. Checkout the rules below before starting the quiz and best of luck!

  • You will get 25 questions in total.
  • You will have only 30 seconds to answer each question.
  • Once you select your answer, it can't be undone.
  • You can't select any option once time goes off.
  • You'll get 4 points on every correct answer.
  • If you leave the quiz or refresh the browser, your score will be set to 0.
  • There is no negative marking.
  • You will get certificate if you score atleast 50%.
  • You need to login to start the quiz.
